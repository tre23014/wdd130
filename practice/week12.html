What is Lochsa Lodge?

Nestled deep in the Clearwater National Forest of Idaho, Lochsa Lodge boasts a rich history that dates back to the early 1920s. Originally established as a stopping point for travelers along the historic Lolo Trail, the lodge has evolved over the decades into a beloved destination for outdoor enthusiasts. Surrounded by majestic mountains and lush forests, the lodge offers a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Its remote location provides visitors with breathtaking views and a serene environment perfect for relaxation and adventure alike.

Lochsa Lodge offers a range of accommodations to suit every traveler's needs, from rustic cabins that echo the charm of days gone by to more modern lodges equipped with all the comforts of home. Guests can enjoy amenities such as a cozy wood-burning stove, comfortable furnishings, and stunning views of the surrounding wilderness. The lodge also features a welcoming main lodge area with a restaurant known for its hearty meals, including locally sourced ingredients and traditional American fare. Whether you're unwinding by the fire or savoring a delicious meal, the lodge provides a warm and inviting atmosphere.

A stay at Lochsa Lodge opens the door to a variety of outdoor activities no matter the season. During the warmer months, guests can embark on exhilarating hikes, kayak along the scenic Lochsa River, or try their hand at fly fishing in the pristine waters. Winter transforms the area into a snowy wonderland, perfect for cross-country skiing and snowshoeing. For those seeking a bit more thrill, white-water rafting on the Lochsa River is a must-try adventure. With its rich array of activities, Lochsa Lodge serves as a gateway to exploring the natural beauty and excitement of Idaho's wilderness year-round.
